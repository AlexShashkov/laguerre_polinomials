unsigned N_roots_ground_truth,
std::vector< fp_t > & roots_to_check,

fp_t & max_absolute_error,
fp_t & max_relative_error )

Compares two vectors of roots.

Root orderings play no role. For each entry in (roots_ground_truth), the closest entry in (roots_to_check) is found and corresponding distance found. Among such distances, the largest will be stored to (max_deviation).

Template Parameters
fp_tThe floating point type.
Parameters
N_roots_to_checkNumber of roots in (roots_to_check).
N_roots_ground_truthNumber of roots in (roots_ground_truth).
roots_to_checkOne should take into account only first (N_roots_to_check) roots here.
roots_ground_truthOne should take into account only first (N_roots_ground_truth) roots here.
max_absolute_errorHere the greatest among the smallest deviations of the roots in (roots_to_check) and (roots_ground_truth) will be placed.
max_relative_errorThe maximum relative error.
Returns
Negative return values may mean internal implementation error.

Generates a test polynomial of any given degree P.

The polynomial is represented both in the form of roots, e.g. (x-roots[0])*(x-roots[1])*(quadratic polynomial with no real roots), as well as by its coefficients, e.g. (coefficients[4]=1)*x^4 + coefficients[3]*x^3 + coefficients[2]*x^2 + coefficients[1]*x + coefficients[0]. The highest-degree coefficient always equals 1.

Template Parameters
fp_tThe floating point type.
exponentThe exponent of the floating point type for bignum.
mantissaThe mantissa of the floating point type for bignum.
Parameters
PPolynomial degree.
N_pairs_of_complex_rootsHow many pairs of complex conjugate roots to introduce.
N_clustered_rootsHow many clustered roots to introduce; all the clustered roots are real.
N_multiple_rootsHow many multiple roots to introduce; all multiple roots are real.
max_distance_between_clustered_rootsMaximal distance between the closest of the clustered roots.
root_sweep_lowLow boundary of real roots; imaginary parts of complex conjugate roots are in the same range.
root_sweep_highHigh boundary of real roots; imaginary parts of complex conjugate roots are in the same range.
rootsStorage where to put the roots; size should exceed P-1.
coefficientsStorage where to put the coefficients; size should exceed P.
Returns
The actual number of different real roots placed into the vector (roots) (complex roots are not placed there). Negative return values may mean internal implementation error.
